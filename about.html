About CAMI

CAMI is a personal safety platform combining a wearable bodycam, context-aware AI, and secure cloud storage. It empowers users in high-risk situations with early escalation detection and visible recording — without compromising privacy. CAMI is not a surveillance tool, but a smart safety device, always under the user’s control.
